Key Headwinds & Investment Risks

While promising, the investment carries significant risks that require careful monitoring and mitigation strategies.

📜 Regulatory Hurdles

Evolving aggregator policies, like take rate caps (e.g., 5-8% in Maharashtra), pose a direct threat to QPo's 24% margin and overall profitability.

⚔️ Intense Competition

The market is dominated by well-funded players (Ola, Uber, Rapido) who are now adopting similar fixed-pay driver models, diluting QPo's key differentiator.

⚙️ Operational Complexity

Rapidly scaling from 4 to 50+ vehicles while maintaining high utilization and positive unit economics is a significant execution challenge.

⛽ High Fuel Costs & The EV Imperative

QPo's implied fuel cost per km is significantly higher than benchmarks. A strategic and rapid transition to a cost-efficient Electric Vehicle (EV) fleet is not just an opportunity but a critical necessity for long-term financial sustainability.
